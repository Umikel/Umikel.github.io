Welcome To Ungogo Local Government Area

Ungogo is one of the Local Government Area in kano state, Its Secretariat are in the town of Ungogo to the north of the city of Kano.It has an area of 204 km2 and a population of 369,657 at the 2006 census.

The areaâ€™s populace made up of members of the Hausa/Fulani ethnic sub division. The Hausa language is commonly spoken in the LGA while the religion of Islam is extensively practiced in the area. Notable landmarks in Ungogo LGA include the Dausara Primary Health Care Center. The postal code of the area is 700105.

Ungogo LGA sits on a total area of 204 square kilometres and has an average temperature of 33 degrees centigrade. The LGA witnesses two major seasons which are the dry and the rainy seasons with average humidity levels in the area put at 28 percent.

Rummawa Primary School project has reached its completion phase, Rummawa is a school that had generated wide media outrage within and beyond the state.
