Welcome To Indigene Certificate Registration Portal

The Ungogo Indigene Certificate Application Process

Clink On Start Register.

Fill The Application Form.

Print Acknowledgment Slip.

Process To Ungogo L.G.A Secretariat.

DOCUMENT REQUIRED

Applicant'S Photo Passport with white background.

National Identity Number (NIN).


GUIDELINES

Applicant Must be from Ungogo Local Government Area by born.

Applicant Must be from 5 years to above.

Applicant under 15 years must come with parent/guardian.

NOTE

The Certificate is not transferable, it remain the property of the Secretariat which reserves the right invail it if further screening reveals.

Secretariat open from Monday to Friday 10:00am To 4:00pm.
