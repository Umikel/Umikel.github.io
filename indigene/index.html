Welcome To Indigene Certificate Registration Portal

The Ungogo Indigene Certificate Application Process

Clink On Start Register.

Fill The Application Form.

Pay 3,000 Certificate Fee.

Print Acknowledgment Slip.

Procedure for payment

Step 1:
Click on 'Pay 3,000' it will proceed you to the payment page.

Step 2:
Enter your Name, Email, Phone Number and Click 'Pay Now' and Make a payment fee of Three Thousand Naira (N3,000) Only using Master/Visa Card or Throught Bank Transfer.

Step 3:
After Sucessfull Payment, Return to the portal to complete the Registration and print your Acknowledgment slip to be presented to the Ungogo Secretariat.

Note:
Non-refundable after payment.

Eligibility

Applicant Must be from Ungogo Local Government Area by born.

Applicant Must be from 5 years to above.

Applicant under 15 years must come with parent/guardian.

NOTE

The Certificate is not transferable, it remain the property of the Secretariat which reserves the right invail it if further screening reveals.

Secretariat open from Monday to Friday 10:00am To 4:00pm.

PDF FORM

Applicant can also download/print the form template filled it and visit nearest internet cafe for online Registration.

Documents Required

Applicant's Photo Passport with white background.

National Identity Number (NIN).
