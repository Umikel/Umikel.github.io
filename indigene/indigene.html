Registraton Procedure

  1. Clink On Start Register.
  2. Fill the Form and submit.
  3. After submit the form clink on "Pay 3,000 Certificate Fee".
  4. Make a Payment through ATM/Bank Transfer.
  5. After Sucessfull payment then back to the portal & "click Print Acknowledment Slip.
  6. Print / Download the Acknowledment Slip.
  7. Process to the Local Government Secretariat for collection the Certificate.

Payment Procedure

Step 1:
Clink on "Pay 3,000 Certificate Fee"It will process
you to the Payment Page
Step 2:
Enter your Name, Email, Phone Number and Click "Pay Now" and Make a payment fee of Three Thousand Naira (N3,000) Only using Master/Visa Card or Throught Bank Transfer.
Step 3:
After Sucessfull Payment, Return to the portal & print your Acknowledgment slip to be presented to the Ungogo Secretariat.
