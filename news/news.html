News & Updates

Chairman Engr.Dr Ramat sponsors 20 Students to study IT programmes at Lincoln University, Malaysia.

On Monday, 23 of January 2023 Engr. Dr. Abdullahi Garba Ramat sponsors 20 Students from Ungogo Local Government Area of Kano State to Lincoln University, Malaysia to study different part of IT programmes, they say praising an expert is a must, but we could not find the right words to praise our expert Engr. Abdullahi Garba Ramat, As we brought them to the first two years they will do in the Capital City Abuja, may Allah show us where we will accompany them to Malaysia to complete their remainig two years.

Rummawa Primary School project has reached its completion phase. Rummawa is a school that had generated wide media outrage within and beyond the state

Before Chairman Engr. Ramat visited the town, Rummawa town had no Primary School, their kids only attended school in neighboring town very far away from Rummawa, after Chairman Engr. Ramat visited and seen their situations, immediately descended on the project, secured and released all funds necessary for its completion.

Hundreds of farmers from ungogo Local Government Area benefit from farmers program

Ungogo local Government has trained and equip hundreds of farmers to establish their poultry and fish farms around the Ungogo Local Government Arae.
