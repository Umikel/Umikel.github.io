Student's Scholarships Sources

There are different types of Scholarships and fellowships designed to help students pay for school tuition and other fees by Ungogo Local Government Area Under RAMAT FOUNDATION. Scholarships and grants are types of gift aid that do not have to be repaid.

Beneficiaries

Ungogo Local Government Scholarships is exclusively meant for only Indigenes of the Local government whose parents are, or whose Father is, of Kano,Ungogo Origin.

Eligibility For Scholarships

  1. Applicant must be Indigene of Ungogo Local Government Area.
  2. Applicant must 16 years to above.
  3. Applicant must passed 5 Subject in their SSCE Result Include English and Maths.

Requirement

  1. Primary School Certificate.
  2. O level Result (SSCE) E.g Neco, WAEC
  3. Date Of Birth / Declaration Of Age
  4. Indigene Certificate
  5. National Identity Number (NIN)
  6. Passport Photograph

How To Apply

  1. Click on Purchase Pin Online.
  2. Enter your Name, Email, Phone Number and Click 'Pay Now' and Make a payment fee of Three Thousand Naira Only using Master/Visa Card or Throught Bank Transfer.

  3. After Sucessfull Payment the Pin Number.
  4. The PIN will be sent to your Email.
  5. Then back to the Scholarship portal and click Apply
  6. Fill the form and Click Submit.
